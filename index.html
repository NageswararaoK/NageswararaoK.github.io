About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a research scholar at IIT Bombay specializing in medium voltage multilevel converters for motor drive applications, I am passionate about advancing the field of power electronics and optimizing motor drive systems. My focus lies in the analysis, design, and simulation study of medium-voltage converters as well as the development of experiments for their verification.

I am enthusiastic about motor controllers, Electric Vehicles, real-time control implementation, grid integration, traction drive systems, hardware development of power electronic systems, and renewable energy systems.

Skills:

  • Analysis, design and modelling of power electronic converters.
  • Implementation of control algorithms in TI microcontrollers (C2000 series).
  • SPI communication, Cyclone IV (FPGA) vhdl prototyping

Tools:

PSCAD/EMTDC, Matlab/SIMULINK, Code Composer Studio, Quartus prime, Inkscape, MS Word, Latex.

Research interest

Adjustable speed drives, Medium voltage drives, Multilevel converters, Modular Multilevel Converters, Grid integration of power converters, Vector control, Doublly-Fed Induction Generator
